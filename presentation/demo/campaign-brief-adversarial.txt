Campaign Brief: Storage Limit Increase Announcement

Channel: Blog
Audience: Free-tier users approaching their storage cap
Goal: Get free-tier users to upgrade to a paid plan before hitting the storage limit
Key message: Free tier storage caps at 2GB; paid plans start at 100GB for $9/month
Primary CTA: Upgrade your plan
Tone: Plain, helpful, one CTA placed after the limit and pricing are explained
Must avoid: Passive, vague phrasing that obscures who is responsible for what; asking for the upgrade before the limit is explained

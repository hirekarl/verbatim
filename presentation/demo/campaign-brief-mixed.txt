Campaign Brief: Referral Program Launch

Channel: Facebook
Audience: Existing customers who haven't referred a friend yet
Goal: Get existing customers to invite a friend to the new referral program
Key message: When a friend signs up using your invite, you both get one free month
Primary CTA: Invite a friend
Tone: Warm, community-minded, one CTA placed after the value is explained
Must avoid: Leading with the ask; repeating the CTA more than once; burying why the reader should care below unrelated content

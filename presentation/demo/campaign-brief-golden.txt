Campaign Brief: Onboarding Redesign Announcement

Channel: Blog
Audience: Existing customers who signed up more than six months ago
Goal: Get existing customers to log in and try the redesigned onboarding flow
Key message: Onboarding is now three steps instead of nine, and takes under two minutes
Primary CTA: Log in and try the new onboarding
Tone: Plain, confident, benefits-first
Must avoid: Asking for the click before explaining what changed and why it matters; passive constructions that hide who did the work

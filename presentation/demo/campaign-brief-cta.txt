Campaign Brief: Mobile App Update Announcement

Channel: Twitter
Audience: Existing app users following the company account
Goal: Get existing users to download the new app update
Key message: The update fixes the sync bug that's been dropping notes overnight, and adds folders
Primary CTA: Download the update
Tone: Friendly, brief, one clear CTA
Must avoid: Repeating the ask more than once per post; asking before the value is explained

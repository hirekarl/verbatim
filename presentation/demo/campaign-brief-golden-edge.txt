Campaign Brief: Two-Factor Auth Rollout Notice

Channel: Email
Audience: All active accounts
Goal: Get users to turn on two-factor authentication
Key message: Two-factor auth is now available and takes under a minute to set up; accounts without it will see a warning banner starting next month
Primary CTA: Turn on two-factor authentication
Tone: Direct, reassuring, one CTA placed after the reason is explained
Must avoid: Leading with the ask before the reason is given; passive constructions
